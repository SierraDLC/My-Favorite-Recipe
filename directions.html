Lets Cook Some Eggplant Lasagna!

Directions

  1. Wash 4 medium sized eggplants thoroughly.
  2. Wash spinach and basil thoroughly.
  3. Cut the eggplants into half inch slices and set aside in container for marinating
  4. Roughly chop 6 cloves of garlic.
  5. Dice 1 onion.
  6. Grate the cheese!
  7. Place the chopped garlic and onion into sauce pan with a little oil.
  8. Let the garic and onion simmer on med for 10 minutes.
  9. Add in a large can of crushed tomatoes.
  10. add half a cup of water and half of a small can of tomato paste.
  11. Add the basil.
  12. Let it cook on medium heat for 30 minutes.
  13. Season with salt, pepper, and sugar to taste.
  14. Take the egg plants and place a few into a frying pan. Make sure to not crowd the pan. Sear until slightly brown.
  15. Take some of the sauce and spread it on the bottom of your baking dish.
  16. Place egg plants on the sauce.
  17. Spread the cheese onto the eggplants.
  18. Repeat steps #15-#18 once
  19. Spread your final layer of cheese with some basil and parmesean.
  20. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes or until cheese is slighty browned.
  21. Let it cool and then eat it with gusto!!
