Let's talk about Eggplant Lasagna!

As a person who loves meat and rarely eats lasagna, I never thought my one of my
favorite recipes would be Eggplant Lasagna. Sometimes I like to have my Eggplant deep fried and other times I may pan sear it.
To get the best flavor, marinating the eggplant in tasty herbs and making the sauce from scratch will yield the best results!
And you definitely can't forget the freshly grated cheese! Not only is this a delicious dish and easy to prepare, it's healthy and light!
Perfect for meatless Mondays or if you're looking to cut back on heavy meals.
